Travel Journey


Hi my name is MD Abu Shahman, currently I am living in Lithuania and I love to travel. In 2025 on my Eid Vacation I travel to Denmark. It was an amazing experience. The weather was perfect and the people were very friendly. I visited many beautiful places like Copenhagen, Aarhus, and Odense. The food was delicious, especially the Danish pastries. I also enjoyed visiting the museums and historical sites. Overall, it was a memorable trip and I can't wait to travel again!
